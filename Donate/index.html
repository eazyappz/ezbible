About

I provide the bible free of charge and I will never ask for any of your information, add any advertising or have any pop-ups. I also invite you to borrow/share any of the content without any demand for recognition or payment... it's the holy bible, I didn't write it and the word needs to be shared.
I'm hoping to expand on what I have here by adding more than just the English language King James version. I'm planning on having many languages and a couple of versions in English. As I have a day job, a wife and other commitments I can only do this when I get a chance so I don't have any timelines.

Donate

If you would like to help me spread the word and maintain the site/application then I'll happily accept anything you feel is fair. Don't feel that you have to give anything though, I'm not doing this to make money.
